Who We Are

A family business with Asia in its bones.

Founded in 1989 by Jimmy Chua, Chuanglee is today led by siblings Christopher, Jennisa and Katriya — a family rooted in food, in trade, and in the long, patient work of building real partnerships on both sides of the world.

From a small importing operation in London, we have grown into a fully integrated supply partner: working directly with manufacturers across Thailand, China, Malaysia and Vietnam to bring authentic, consistent, scalable ingredients into UK kitchens — from independent operators to restaurant groups of 50+ sites and Michelin-starred establishments.

We supply ambient, chilled, frozen and fresh categories — full-basket, direct-into-kitchen, with our own fleet servicing London and Greater London multiple times a week.

Partnerships sit at the very core of what we do. We don't just import ingredients — we import the people, the recipes, and the standards behind them. — The Chua Family

Our Heritage

Three decades of building partnerships.

What began as a small London importing business in 1989 is now a fully integrated foodservice ecosystem — but the philosophy hasn't changed: family-run, partnership-led, slow to commit, long to stay.

1989
Chuanglee founded by Jimmy Chua. Begins importing East Asian ingredients into the UK.
2011
Chuanglee Cash & Carry acquired — expanding wholesale reach.
2023
Joins NRF Group; acquires Chinese foodservice business — full ecosystem formed.
2024
First Mini C Asian Pantry retail store opens in Greenwich, London.
2025
Multi-site ordering app launches. Revenue reaches £25M with 450+ customers.
Tomorrow
The next generation continues the work — same family, same standards.
