Suree's Farm · Chachoengsao

Granny Suree's herbs, on a plane every week.

One hour outside Bangkok, our family farm and packing house grows organic Thai produce alongside trusted local farmers — packed to order, flown to the UK weekly. The supply chain is short on purpose: it keeps flavour intact and keeps the work in the hands of people we know.

For decades, Granny Suree has personally overseen the pack house, partnering with local farmers while growing her own organic produce. Everything is packed to order and flown to the UK on a weekly basis — ensuring fresh, high-quality, seasonal produce reaches kitchens at their peak.

  • Cha Plu Leaves — wild pepper leaf, peppery & aromatic
  • Kaffir Lime Leaves — picked, packed, never frozen
  • Calamansi · Sour Mango · Marian Plum — true seasonal rotation
  • Morning Glory · Aubergine Pea · Jujube — restaurant-grade volumes

A 5-day journey.

1

Picked at Dawn

Harvested from the family farm and partner farms in Chachoengsao, Thailand.

2

Packed to Order

Sorted, washed and packed only after an order is placed — no warehouse sitting.

3

Flown Direct

Air-freighted from Bangkok to London Heathrow. Full cold-chain management.

4

UK Clearance

Cleared through Heathrow and transferred to our SE7 London warehouse.

5

Your Kitchen

Delivered direct via our own fleet — multiple times a week across London.

What We Supply

Consistent, seasonal, custom.

Suree's Farm offers three core supply models — consistent weekly staples, seasonal rotation produce, and custom growing for specific restaurant menu requirements. All packed to order, all flown weekly.

01 / Year-round Supply

Weekly staples. Always in stock.

Kaffir lime leaves, cha plu, morning glory and calamansi are grown year-round and available on every weekly flight. Consistent supply, consistent quality.

↗
02 / Seasonal Rotation

True seasonal Thai produce.

Sour green mango, Marian plum, Thai jujube — grown when Thailand grows them, not forced out of season. Rotating weekly based on farm conditions.

↗
03 / Custom

Grown for your menu.

Working with Granny Suree's farmer network, we can source specific varieties to restaurant specification and grow-to-order for consistent volumes.

↗
04 / Ordering

Via account or the Chuanglee app.

Fresh produce integrated into your normal trade order — same delivery, same invoice. Or order via our multi-site ordering app with weekly cut-off.
